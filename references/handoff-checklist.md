# Handoff Checklist

Use this checklist before considering the output complete.

## For design

- Are core roles clearly separated?
- Are workflows written as role-specific flows?
- Are trigger conditions and end conditions clear?
- Can a designer infer page inventory and interaction needs?

## For engineering

- Are modules and responsibilities clear?
- Are the needed structured fields identified?
- Is data lineage from raw source to structured result clear?
- Are state transitions explicit enough to implement?
- Are acceptance criteria concrete enough to verify?

## For testing

- Can main workflows be turned into test scenarios?
- Are major states and transitions testable?
- Are open questions and risks explicitly listed?

## Completion rule

If the output cannot support direct continuation by design and engineering, it is not complete.
